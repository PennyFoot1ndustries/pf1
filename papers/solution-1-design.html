Solution 1 Design

We tend to attempt to develop and maintain our own nomenclature and methodology for design when possible. My first memory of doing such was when I devised a proprietary methodology for the assembly of a Halbach array in university. Once I had developed the conceptual idea of how to assemble the array with the resources available, I enjoyed the act of naming it myself and developing it within my studies. HAAM or Halbach Array Assembly Mechanism was my first such nomenclature and it was made because I was able to figure out the problem of how to keep the magnets steady and correctly oriented during assembly with the help of my ideas. It is what drew me to what I do now; progressive engineering design. Licensing such as CERN open hardware types are what I am into now. I don’t design this way to make money. It is simply what I believe science is all about. You're never done with a design. It can always interact with a new problem set. And be measured in a standardized fashion. Over a scale of 0-1. We ignore the negative spectrum of the number line but that is not to say we couldn’t explore the methodology as -1<—>1 where a solution 1 design would be perfection of design with respect to problem set solution and a value of -1 would be spontaneous disassembly of one of more of the major parts of focus in regards to the problem set of interest. I would note that a solution 1 design does not assume one has attained perfection and can retire. Instead it means here that the problem set that was presented has been fully solved via the applied design within the scope of the project problem set. Another problem set could always be applied to the design to test its value on a -1<—>1 scale with respect to how well the interpreter believes the problem set has been solved. Example case: Tim wants to fix his ladder. He has $400, some rubber bands, some scrap metal, a fully equipped welding apparatus, and various other resources that would confuse the example. He fixes it by spot welding the broken ladder. It later breaks again. Then he buys a new one. Then the example is measured in a solution 1 prototype scaling scope. What is the interpreter’s value? Well anyone can be an interpreter and each individual person would bring bias to the measured value but lets imagine we have 3 interpreters; Sam, Bill, and Tess. Sam sais 1. Bill sais 1.77. Tess sais 0.4. The example doesn’t go into each of the individual interpreter’s design review reports and experience, etc, etc. But Bill doesn’t understand the exercise. Sam has an easy scale. Tess is measuring that the rubber bands were not utilized and is a stickler for offsets of embodied energy in procured resources.
