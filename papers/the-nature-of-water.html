The Nature of Water

when considering the embodied water use of a process it is important to consider the nature of water within the system of the stratosphere. water is conserved, always, within this system. to lose water to processing waste is to discover it across the planet forming within a weather system that gives preciptation. that isnt to discount localized lack of water (drought, water shortage) but besides drought, water scarcity is largely exasserbated by human factors.
